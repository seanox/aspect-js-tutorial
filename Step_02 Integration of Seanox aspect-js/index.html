Project Details (Example)

Summer House

Simple elegance in clinker and wood.

Summer house with two bedrooms, two bathrooms, living room, dining room, kitchen, sauna and fireplace for up to 6 people. Underfloor heating/gas floor heating.

The spacious living area is equipped with noble real wood. On the ground floor there is a kitchen, a shower room with sauna and on the first floor two bedrooms with bath and toilet.
